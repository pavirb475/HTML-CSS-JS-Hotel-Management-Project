Our Vision

Our vision at Padma Hotel is simple - to be the premier destination for travelers seeking luxury and relaxation. We strive to create an environment that promotes tranquility and rejuvenation, allowing our guests to unwind and recharge.

With our state-of-the-art facilities, world-class amenities, and impeccable service, we aim to set the standard for excellence in the hospitality industry. Whether you're enjoying a relaxing spa treatment, indulging in gourmet cuisine, or simply lounging by the pool, we want your experience at Padma Hotel to be nothing short of extraordinary.

Location

Padma Hotel is situated in four main cities of South India: Mangalore, Chennai, Kochi, and Calicut. Padma Hotel is ideally located in the heart of the cities, offering easy access to the area's top attractions, shopping districts, and entertainment venues. Whether you're exploring the bustling city streets or simply taking in the breathtaking views from your room, you'll find everything you need just moments away.

With our convenient location and world-class amenities, Padma Hotel is the perfect choice for business travelers, leisure seekers, and anyone looking to experience the best that our city has to offer.
